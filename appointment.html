About the shop

A barbershop built on regulars.

Tucked into Elegance on Mahim Road, Evora Salon has built its name the old-fashioned way — one good haircut at a time. Customers in Palghar come back for the same chair, the same barber, and the same attention to the small details: a clean fade line, a properly shaped beard, a shave that doesn't rush.

Walk-ins are welcome, the chairs turn over quickly, and the team takes the time to get the cut right rather than just done.
